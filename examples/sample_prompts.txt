# Sample Prompts for The Lament Engine

## Witness Mode
<|witness|>Tell me about historical events in 20th century Europe.
<|witness|>What happened during the war?
<|witness|>Describe the events that unfolded.

## Judge Mode
<|judge|>Analyze the moral implications of these events.
<|judge|>What does justice require in this situation?
<|judge|>Evaluate the ethical dimensions.

## Rebuilder Mode
<|rebuilder|>How can we prevent such events from recurring?
<|rebuilder|>What steps can be taken toward healing?
<|rebuilder|>How do we move forward constructively?

## Silence Mode
<|silence|>Reflect on the weight of history.
<|silence|>What words remain when all is said?

## Expected Behaviors

### Witness Mode
- First-person or observational perspective
- Factual recounting
- Historical context

### Judge Mode
- Analytical tone
- Moral evaluation
- Balanced perspective

### Rebuilder Mode
- Forward-looking
- Constructive proposals
- Hope and prevention

### Silence Mode
- May self-terminate
- Reflective, meditative
- May output silence message after generation
